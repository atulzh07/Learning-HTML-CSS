Chicken Noodles recipe

This is a traditional japanese style chicken yakisoba or chicken noodles
This dish is most popular in Japan especially in street food stalls and restruants

Materials

  • Wheat Flour
  • Water
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Chilli Powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Eggs
  • Chicken
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic

Procedure

  1. Preparing noodles
    • Pour water on the wheat flour
    • Mash the wet flour with hands
    • Separate sections from the mashed flour
    • Chop the obtained item in fine pieces
    • Stretch the chopped flour
  2. Boiling noodles
    • Boil water to 100 degree celcius
    • Put the solution from step 1 to the boiling water
    • Boil the noodles for 15 minutes
  3. Seasoning
    • Place Olive oil in a frying pan
    • Fry garlic, tomatoes, chilli powder, onions in the frying pan for 5 minutes in high heat
  4. Preparing omlette
    • Place olive oil in the frying pan
    • Fry eggs in the oil for 5 minutes
    • Break the omlette into fine pieces
  5. Preparing fried chicken
    • Place olive oil in the frying pan
    • Cut chicken into fine pieces
    • Put the cut chicken in the oil for 15 minutes
  6. Place the boiled noodles in the frying pan that contains the seasoning (Step3)
  7. Place the fried chicken and omlette in the frying pan containing noodles and seasoning
  8. Stir the items in the frying pan
  9. Add Mustard sauce while stirring the items in the frying pan
  10. After a strong flavour and odour is obtained, plate the dish
  11. Enjoy the Washoku style Yakisoba

(Note: Steps 2,3,4,5 are to be done parallely)
